Open Online Class Link

Simple script that can help you to open online class links according to your timetable. This is for people who join recurring meetings i.e. the same meeting link multiple times for different periods. This is not just for school students, it works also for job meetings. Just adjust the timetable according to your need. There is no need of searching for link, looking at the timetable, checking the day, time, etc. The script will do that for you.
